Intent Statement: Rescue and secure imprisoned POWs and extract from the area.

1. Concept of the Operation

To maneuver to the suitable meeting position (SMP) in preperation for negotiation with the local Al-Qaeda militia for the release of our POWs and/or the best (only) location for extraction, although the local tribal warlords may also have the latter information. Detours may be made along the way to consult these warlords. All negotiation tactics are up to the mission commander's discretion, although it is recommend that a negotiator is used to communicate with hostiles. Our supply trucks are our most powerful assets in negotiations with the Al-Qaeda. After contact with the local Al-Qaeda militia, proceed with the mission commander's plan to obtain the POWs and extract from the area.
