Intent Statement: Secure at least 2 MTVR supply vehicles (NOT transport vehicles) and extract from the area.

1. Concept of the Operation

To maneuver to the suitable meeting position (SMP) in preperation for negotiation with the US Army forces for the transfer of supply trucks and/or the best (only) location for extraction, although the local tribal warlords may also have the latter information. Detours may be made along the way to consult these warlords. All negotiation tactics are up to the mission commander's discretion, although it is recommend that a negotiator is used to communicate with hostiles. Our POWs are our most powerful assets in negotiations with the US. After contact with the local US Army forces, proceed with the mission commander's plan to obtain the supply trucks and extract from the area.
